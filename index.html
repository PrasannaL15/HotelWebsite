Tourist Places to Visit near Sea Nest

Ratnagiri, a coastal city in the Indian state of Maharashtra, is surrounded by a plethora of breathtaking tourist destinations. Some of the most popular attractions near Ratnagiri include the majestic Ratnadurg Fort, the serene Ganapatipule Beach, the stunning Jaigad Fort, the historic Thibaw Palace, the picturesque Pawas Village, and the fascinating Swayambhu Ganpati Temple. Additionally, tourists can also explore the tranquil beaches of Bhatye, Guhagar, and Ganeshghule, take a boat ride in the tranquil waters of the Mandovi River, and enjoy a scenic drive through the lush green hills of Amboli Ghat.
