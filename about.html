SEA NEST

Sea Nest is a sprawling premium rustic bungalow, boasting two generously-sized air-conditioned bedrooms with king-sized beds, along with an additional queen-sized bedroom. This charming villa also features a capacious living area, a well-equipped kitchen, and a separate dining space. Perched atop a gentle hill in the tranquil village of Nevare, Sea Nest offers guests a breathtaking panoramic vista of the idyllic sea. For those seeking a serene retreat to unwind and immerse themselves in the splendor of nature, Sea Nest stands as an ideal haven, showcasing the mesmerizing natural beauty of the Konkan region.
